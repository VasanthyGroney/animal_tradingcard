Peacock

A peacock that's spreading its feathers is trying to attract a mate.

  • Scientific name: Pavo cristatus.
  • Average Size: 98 inches.
  • Average lifespan: 15 to 20 years.
  • Habitat: Forest areas, farms, and agricultural fields.

Peacocks take years to develop colorful tail feathers. Their Crests are Actually important sensors. Peacocks regularly fly short distances in order to escape to a tree branch for protection from predators or to nest at night.
